Which sections live on the Daily Command Center?

Select all the modules you want on the page — you can deselect any that don't fit

1

Status Header

Day counter (Day 14 of 90), date, current streak, summer progress %, week number. Always visible at top.

2

Today's Mission

The 3-5 most important tasks for today across all areas. Your daily north star — what must get done.

3

Goal Cascade

Summer goals → month goals → week goals → today's tasks. See exactly how today connects to the bigger picture.

4

Habit Tracker

Daily habit checklist with streak counters. Morning protocol, fitness, journaling, cold shower, etc.

5

Daily Schedule / Time Blocks

Hourly or block-based view of the day. Deep work blocks, study blocks, breaks, meals. Your Monk Mode protocol.

6

Academics Module

What you're studying, current topic/chapter, study time logged today, weekly study goal progress.

7

Build / Product Module

Current sprint tasks, product milestones, features shipped, what you're working on today.

8

Fitness Log

Today's workout, weekly training plan, body metrics, PRs. Gym, running, whatever your protocol is.

9

Mind / Mental

Journaling prompt, mood/energy rating, meditation log, reading log. The mental side of Monk Mode.

10

Stats & Streaks

Habit streak calendar (GitHub contribution graph style), weekly completion rates, long-term trend charts.

11

Monk Mode Protocol

Morning and evening routine checklist. Fixed steps you follow every single day — your operating system.
